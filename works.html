SyllaScan

SyllaScan allows users to upload a PDF copy of their syllabus and then scans through the document to find all due dates. Once scanning, dates are exported into Google Calendars so users can keep track of all dates. Our features include summarizing course structure, professor contact details and more. Note: project is not uploaded on GitHUb yet. Problems arise from inconsistent Syllabus structure, leading to difficulties.
